Tooltip

A text hint that floats beside any element, inspired by Ant Design's Tooltip. Apply the [lTooltip] directive to a target; it shows on hover and keyboard focus (or click), renders in a fixed layer that escapes ancestor overflow clipping, flips when it would overflow the viewport, and points an arrow at the trigger. Twelve placements, custom color, open/close delays, and Esc-to-dismiss are built in.
