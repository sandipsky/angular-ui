Sidebar & Header

Empty layout shells. <l-sidebar> is a 200px rail that collapses to 70px on desktop and becomes a slide-in drawer with a backdrop below 768px; both states are two-way bindable ([(collapsed)], [(mobileOpen)]) and its toggle() picks the right one per viewport. <l-header> is a 40px bar whose hamburger emits menuToggle — wire it to the sidebar's toggle().
