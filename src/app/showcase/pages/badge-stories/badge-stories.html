Badge

A count badge applied as a directive — drop lBadge on any element (button, icon, avatar) and a bubble is positioned at its top-right corner. Inputs are prefixed to avoid clashing with native attributes: lBadgeColor, lBadgeSize, lBadgeOverflowCount, [lBadgeShowZero], [lBadgeDynamic], and the (lBadgeClick) output. Hides at zero unless lBadgeShowZero.
