Stepper

A progress indicator for multi-step flows, styled after Mantine's stepper — the indicator sits inline with a title/description and connecting rails run between steps. Pass an array of {{ '{' }} title, description, icon?, error?, completed? {{ '}' }} steps and the active index. Steps before the active one show a checkmark in the success color; steps flagged error turn red with an alert mark. Drive active from clickable steps or from external buttons via [(active)]. Lay it out horizontal (default) or vertical.
